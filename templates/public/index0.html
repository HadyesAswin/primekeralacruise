Our Story at PrimeKeralaCruise

**PrimeKeralaCruise** is dedicated to crafting **unforgettable backwater experiences** in Alappuzha. We offer a fleet of **premium houseboats**, private **shikara rides**, and thrilling **speedboats**, ensuring we have the **perfect escape** for every traveler.

We are a local team passionate about sharing the authentic beauty and culture of Kerala, providing high-quality service and **traditional, fresh Kerala cuisine** on every journey. Our commitment is to your comfort and peace.
